PROJECT DESCRIPTION

The Future of Work Testimonials were commissioned from "testimonial actors" on Fiverr.com. The actors we hired were not given a script. Instead, they were presented with a series of questions about dream jobs, automation, the sharing economy, and the future of work. To our knowledge, the following responses reflect the personal views and experiences of these workers.
