The Future of Work

by Anxious to Make

2016

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

In The Future of Work, Anxious to Make aggregates over 100 results from a survey asking participants about the sharing economy, labor, bosses, and the future.

This survey was distributed at two different galleries: V2_ in Rotterdam, NL, for the show “The Gig is Up: How New Technologies Are Reshaping The Future Of Work”; and B4BEL4B in Oakland, CA, for Anxious to Make’s solo show “Side Gig”. Responses to the survey were also commissioned from cloud workers on Fiverr.com and Amazon Mechanical Turk.
