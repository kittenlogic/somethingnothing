Ultimate Fantasy

2022

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Ultimate Fantasy is a collection of short stories, poetry and art generated by Queer AI, an artificial intelligence trained on a corpus of queer theatre.

Prompts used to generate the texts were adapted from works of queer theory, a queer history of computing, queer spirituality, and some original material written by the artist. All "rare" images were generated using excerpts of the AI generated texts from the collection. Audio was created with AI voiceover software.
