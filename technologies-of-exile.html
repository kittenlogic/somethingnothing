Technologies of Exile

2024

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Technologies of Exile is a series of AI-generated images that revisits the Cuban rafter crisis of the 1990s, when approximately 35,000 people fled the island on makeshift rafts built from whatever materials they could find â€” tires, styrofoam, tar, cloth, sheet metal, wood, and other scraps of a society defined by scarcity. Against the backdrop of an economic embargo and limited resources, these rafts were more than just a means of escape; they were acts of necessity and defiance.

This project is not a representation of history. Instead, it is an exploration of how humans adapt technologies to limitation and how technologies fail. By attempting to imagine rafts that could have existed, the series draws parallels between the human ingenuity that defied systems of control and scarcity, with artistic creativity and the AI's inability to fully grasp the story it is trying to tell.
