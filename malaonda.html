PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Mala Onda is a love letter to Miami and invitation to create a guide for surviving the future of climate change and accelerated capitalism. Made as Anxious to Make.

Mala Onda was commissioned by Temporary Art Review for Common Field Convening 2016 | Miami from October 23-25, 2016. The project was embedded in an essay by theorist and curator Joshua Simon published on temporaryartreview.com and on Common Field | Research and was part of a broader segment on the impacts of accelerated capitalism.
