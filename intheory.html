in theory

infinite, self-generating philosophy

2012- (in progress)

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

In theory is a program capable of generating an infinite philosophical text based on recursive google search results originated by the phrase, “in theory”. As the program learns about theory, it attempts to reconcile the differences between theory and practice, until all possibile combinations of theory vs/and/or practice are exhausted and/or a new theory about theory and practice is put forth. The mockup below features a prerecorded sample of what to expect from the text itself. I apologize for the poorly synced audio to (scrolling) text. This is only a test.
