PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Experiments with multimodal AI. Titles, descriptions, and text prompts are 100% AI-generated from "Ultimate Fantasy", a collection of short stories and poems written by Queer AI, a GPT-2 machine learning model trained on 50,000+ conversational pairs derived from scripts in queer theatre.
