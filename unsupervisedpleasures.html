PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Unsupervised Pleasures is an archive of Emily's experiments with LLMs and generative AI models. It is also a DIY community practice space that grew out of Emily's research practice and a longing to collaborate more with people interested in working with machine learning, storytelling, and their own cultural archives.
